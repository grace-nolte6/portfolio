Visual Techniques

Visual techniques encompass various artistic mediums, including drawing, painting and more.
These techniques play a crucial role in expressing ideas, emotions, and narratives visually.
They allow artists to communicate thoughts and experiences in a way that words alone cannot capture.
Drawing, for instance, conveys form, structure, and texture, while painting adds color and mood.
These techniques enable artists to tap into their creativity, provide a visual language that transcends cultural and language barriers, and evoke strong emotional responses from viewers.
Visual techniques are an essential means of artistic expression, as they engage and stimulate the senses, provoke thought, and inspire a deeper understanding and appreciation of the world around us.

All of the Visual Techniques projects below were created by me using mixed media.

The artworks titles and the mediums used (left to right): All's Good And Well... It Seems -Fineliner and Ink. A Starry Night -Gouche paint. The Toucan -Oil Pastel. The Wendigo -Graphite pencil.
